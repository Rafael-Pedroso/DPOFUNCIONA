== Description ==

Botsonic AI chatbot builder makes it effortless to centralise all your data on your website, no-code. 
Make your knowledge-base chat ready by training ChatGPT on your own data.

With this plugin, you can embed your Botsonic chatbot on your Wordpress site.

A Quick installation guide can be found here: [Botsonic](https://docs.botsonic.ai/docs/wordpress-integration "Botsonic wordpress plugin installation guide")

Note: We're Botsonic, a part of company Writesonic, and this is our own official integration, allowing WordPress users to use our services. It is built by our company and uses our API.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I initialize my chatbot? =
Goto https://bot.writesonic.com and create a new chatbot.
Goto Bot Settings section under Share section you can find a embedable code. Grab the token from there.
Enter your Botsonic Token that you got from the previous step on Botsonic website.
Save the settings.
